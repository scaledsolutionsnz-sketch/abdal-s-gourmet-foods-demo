Our Story

Passion on a Plate,
Every Time

Abdal's Gourmet Foods started with a simple belief: that great street food should be made properly. No compromises, no shortcuts — just real ingredients, authentic recipes, and flavours that make people stop and come back for more.

We're a food truck rooted in Dunedin, serving gourmet meals to locals who appreciate food that's been crafted with genuine care. Every dish on our menu is a reflection of the bold, vibrant flavours we love — and we're proud to share them with this city.

Whether you're grabbing lunch on the go or ordering for the family, you can count on Abdal's for food that hits the spot every single time.
